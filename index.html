About Dr Mastakov

Dr Mikhail Mastakov is a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (RACS), a member of General Surgeons Australia (GSA), and a member of the Australian & New Zealand Metabolic and Obesity Surgery Society (ANZMOSS).

He brings extensive experience in bariatric and general surgery, with a strong focus on safe, evidence-based care and patient education.
