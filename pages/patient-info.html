Fees & Payment

Surgery costs vary depending on the procedure, complexity, hospital, and anaesthetist involved. Total costs typically include:

  • Surgeon fees
  • Anaesthetist fees
  • Surgical assistant fees
  • Hospital fees
  • Equipment and supplies
  • Pathology and imaging

Private Health Insurance

If you have private health insurance that covers surgical procedures, please bring your insurance card to your first appointment. We can provide information about your coverage.

Medicare & DVA

Please inform us of any Medicare or DVA benefits you're entitled to, as these may apply to your procedure.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I schedule my appointment?

You can request an appointment at any time. The practice will contact you within 48 hours to confirm.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring:

  • Photo ID and Medicare card
  • Private health insurance card (if applicable)
  • Any relevant medical records or imaging
  • A list of current medications
  • Completed patient questionnaire

Can I bring a family member or support person?

Yes, we encourage you to bring a support person to your consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

Please contact the practice directly to discuss payment options.

Do you offer payment plans?

Please discuss payment arrangements with our reception team when you book your appointment.

How long will my consultation take?

Initial consultations typically take 30-45 minutes. This allows time for a thorough assessment and discussion.

What happens after surgery?

Post-operative care is an important part of your surgical journey. Dr Mastakov and the team will provide clear instructions and arrange follow-up appointments as needed.

For medical emergencies, what should I do?

Call 000 immediately.

For questions or concerns about your surgery outside normal business hours, you can call 07 4148 7082.
