Abdominoplasty (Tummy Tuck)

What is Abdominoplasty?

Abdominoplasty, commonly called a tummy tuck, removes excess abdominal skin and tightens underlying muscles to restore a smoother, firmer abdominal contour. This procedure is often performed for patients who have excess skin from weight loss or post-pregnancy changes.

About the Procedure

During abdominoplasty:

  • Excess abdominal skin is removed
  • Underlying abdominal muscles may be tightened
  • The remaining skin is repositioned for a smoother contour
  • Incisions are typically placed horizontally below the bikini line

Who May Benefit?

Abdominoplasty may be considered for patients with:

  • Excess abdominal skin after significant weight loss
  • Loose skin after pregnancy
  • Weak or separated abdominal muscles
  • Skin redundancy affecting contour

Your Consultation

During your consultation with Dr Mastakov, you will:

  • Discuss your goals and expectations
  • Have a physical examination
  • Review the procedure and recovery
  • Understand risks and benefits
  • Have all your questions answered
