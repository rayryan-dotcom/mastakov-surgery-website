Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

Dr Mastakov Surgery respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and postcode
  • Medical Information: Information provided in appointment request forms and questionnaires
  • Website Analytics: Information about how you use our site (via Cloudflare Analytics)
  • Cookies: Information stored in your browser for site functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Contact you regarding your appointment request
  • Provide you with surgical care and follow-up
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for secure data transmission.

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Our web hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages)
  • Regulatory bodies when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to certain uses of your information
